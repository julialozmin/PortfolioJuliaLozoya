Hi, I am Julia

Front-End developer based in Italy

I am passionate about marketing and business operations and I have a background in Business Administration and Advertising. I love challenges and learning new things, so during the pandemic I decided to give coding a try. I had created some websites using templates in the past, but I often couldn't make some changes because the HTML file was really intimidating. So when I saw an introductory course online during the lockdown, I decided to join.

I found it really interesting and inspiring; with some HTML, CSS and Javascript I made so much progress and saw great potential. However, life was resumed, and I set coding aside until 2023, when I decided to change the direction of my career and study to become a full-time developer.

Coding has been not just logical but surprisingly creative, as there can be different solutions to the same issue or task. I find it inspiring; it leads me down rabbit holes of perfectionism and, of course, moments of frustration.

I am detail-oriented, driven and flexible and, although I am autonomous, I thrive as a team player. A natural problem-solver, I enjoy thinking outside the box to find new options and improve workflows.

When I am not at the computer, learning Italian or exploring new subjects in Udemy or Domestika, you will find me indulging in my favourite “granny hobbies”- sewing my own clothes, knitting or painting. I often multitask these activities with listening to my favourite podcasts (with various topics such as news, art, pop culture or crimes) or talking with family and friends over the phone, especially important to me as an expat with loved ones far away.

I have a passion for hiking and I am very fortunate because Abruzzo, the region I am living in, is famous for its nature, with multiple National Parks and Reserves, which I love to explore during the weekends.
